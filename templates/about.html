About Task Tracker

Welcome to Task Tracker! This simple app helps you stay organized by tracking your daily tasks. You can:

  • Add new tasks with due dates.
  • Mark tasks as completed or keep them pending.
  • Edit or delete tasks as needed.
  • Filter tasks based on their status.

Built with Flask for the backend, HTML/CSS for the frontend, and SQLite as the database, Task Tracker is designed for simplicity and efficiency.
